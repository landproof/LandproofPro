LandProof — AI-Powered Property Trust Infrastructure for Africa
LandProof is an AI-powered property trust and land governance platform designed to eliminate land fraud, enforce development compliance, and preserve critical property records across Nigeria and Africa.
Built with Abuja (FCT) as its first implementation ground, LandProof functions as a property operating system, integrating land verification, development control, building approval tracking, secure document archiving, fraud risk scoring, and government registry synchronization into a single trusted platform.
🌍 Why LandProof Exists
Land fraud, forged documents, illegal developments, lost government files, and opaque approval processes have cost citizens, banks, developers, and governments billions. LandProof addresses these systemic failures by introducing automation, AI verification, compliance intelligence, and tamper-proof digital records into land administration and property transactions.
